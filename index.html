Mesh Security Cages & Enclosures

Security is paramount in any data center, and our mesh security cages and provide a robust layer of protection. Our enclosures are made from high-quality materials and are designed to secure sensitive equipment while providing ample ventilation. These security cages can be custom-made to your specifications, ensuring maximum security for your valuable assets.

Cage Privacy

Privacy screening serves as a visual barrier for data center enclosures, effectively shielding sensitive equipment and data from external observation. This measure ensures that the inner workings of the data center remain out of sight, accessible only to authorized personnel, which is essential for maintaining operational confidentiality. Such screening not only reinforces security protocols but also contributes to a more organized and professional appearance by obscuring cables and hardware. We have developed a unique cage design for these purposes.

High Security Cage Design and Construction

In addition to our mesh security cages, we also offer high security cage design and construction. These solutions provide an extra level of security for your most critical assets. Designed to meet strict security standards, our cages are robust, durable, and resistant to physical threats.
