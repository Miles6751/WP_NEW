Cage Electric & Products CO, specializes in providing efficient and cost-effective power whips for data centers. We understand that powering the equipment in your data center is critical, and we are here to help you save time and money during the construction or renovation process.

About

The General Manager and owner has been a licensed N.J. electrical contractor for 25 years, with a total of 35 years of experience in the electrical industry. In addition, he spent 5 years as a purchasing agent at the largest electrical supply house in N.J. His extensive credentials and exceptional work ethic led him to become the President of the State Electrical Association. Our team is composed of highly trusted and skilled professionals, each bringing a wealth of experience to the table. Their expertise and dedication ensure exceptional service and results.

Strategic Advantages

We have a New Jersey Licensed Electrical Owner (License #14236) on-site during production. Preassembly is consistently more cost-efficient, constructed by skilled labor in a controlled environment. We partner with you to ensure that your scheduling and delivery dates are met.

Reliability

Our assemblies, final connections and testing are under the supervision of a licensed electrician and utilize UL approved components. They are assembled in our facility to ensure a reduction in lead time as well as cost.

Power Whips 24/7

Our products are custom-made to meet your specific needs, and we also maintain a stock of items at your request, ensuring you have immediate access in case of emergencies. We provide same day shipping.
Shipping method options: box, barrel, skid, cart. 

Savings and Performance

Our power whips are pre-manufactured cables that arrive at your data center ready to be installed. This eliminates the need for on-site cable assembly, which can be a time-consuming and labor-intensive process. By choosing our pre-manufactured power whips, you can save significant time and labor costs.

Cost-Effective

With our pre-manufactured design and customization options, we can help you save time and money while ensuring reliable power delivery to your equipment. We understand that cost is always a consideration, and we ensure that while maintaining a high level of quality, all our products are built in accordance with UL inspection guidelines.

Customization and Efficiency

Tailoring power delivery systems to reduce waste, ensure reliability, and optimize performance within your data center environment.
